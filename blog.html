why i am christian

07.06.19

But before you stop reading this, I'm letting you know it has not been a smooth ride at all. And it's definitely not meant to be. I've gone through phases where I thought Christianity was stupid, a bunch of fake people who made up something in a weak attempt to explain things they weren't smart enough to understand. One of the big things I always questioned was why do I need God if I can just put in X amount of work and get the results I want? Why do I need God if I'm happy right now? Why should I believe in a God if bad things continue to happen? These questions, which are the reason why I've doubted Christianity so many times, are the exact reason why I can confidently say that I am a Christian and believe in God.

It's through all these doubts and my desire to find an answer to these questions that I've realized that the answers for all these questions are found in Christianity, in the Bible. And through this, I am able to make sense of reality...
